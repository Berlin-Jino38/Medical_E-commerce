About Us

Established in 2002 by Mr. Raj, Deepti Medicals has been proudly serving the community of Karungal for over two decades. Our commitment to providing top-notch healthcare products and services has earned us the reputation of being one of the best medical stores in the area. Conveniently located opposite the Karungal Bus Stand, we have been a reliable partner in your health journey.
